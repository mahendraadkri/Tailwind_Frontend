Why Us ?


With over three decades of experience in Nepal's automotive industry, Suzuki proudly holds the title of being The Number 1 selling 4-Wheeler brand in the country. Few brands can match the level of trust and confidence that Nepali customers have in Suzuki. Suzuki's extensive lineup of SUVs and compact cars caters to the diverse needs of Nepalese customers, making it the go-to choice. Suzuki, a renowned Japanese car brand with a global reputation for its technical innovations, has consistently delivered award-winning designs and cutting-edge engine technology. The remarkable Suzuki lineup in Nepal is further enhanced with the introduction of the iconic "Jimny" and the modern urban SUV, "Fronx," complementing the existing Grand Vitara and Brezza models. This solidifies Suzuki's position as a PROMINENT SUV BRAND in Nepal.
