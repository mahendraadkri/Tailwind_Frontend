CG | Moto Corp, the automobile division of Chaudhary Group is the pioneer in the Nepalese automobile industry for the past 30 years. CG | Moto Corp is the sole distributor of Suzuki Motors Corporation (Japan) and Maruti Suzuki India Limited (India).
Suzuki brands of power-packed new generation cars include Alto 800, Spresso, Wagon R, Celerio, Swift, Baleno, Ciaz, Vitara Brezza, Scross, Ertiga, XL6 and Eeco Series. The company boosts the widest product range among any brands available in the country. The products range from economy to city cars to mid-range and to utility vehicles. The Brand has well established itself in Nepal and Globally in terms of technology, reliability, efficiency, and ease of maintenance
CG | Moto Corp has 38 Sales Outlets and boasts of 46 State of the art After-sales Service Centers spread across Nepal. With the widest reach among any brands available, CG | Moto Corp provides the easiest reach for customers to access its sales and service facility across the country. CG | Moto Corp has not only spread the reach but also ensured that the cost of ownership is economical with the lowest cost and the easiest available spare part which added with maximum warranty coverage providing unmatched assurance.
CG | Moto Corp employs the largest and best quality human resource dedicated and motivated to provide the best service to the customer starting from the day he/she enters the Suzuki facility and every day to make his/ her Suzuki experience the pleasant one.
CG | Moto Corp is driven by a customer-centric approach with excellence as a key management mantra. This along with dedication and discipline the company has established Suzuki as “The No.1 Selling Car in Nepal” and “the Car with the Highest Resale Value” the fact evident with more than 50000 Suzuki vehicles plying the Nepali Road today.
